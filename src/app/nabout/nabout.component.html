Hi, we’re Out of Mana.

We’re a Horde casual raiding guild on Emerald Dream-Terenas. We’ve been around since The Burning Crusade so we’re pretty well established here. We’re a mix of dungeon enthusiasts, achievement hunters, mount hoarders, amateur pvpers, reputation farmers and reliable raiders. We raid twice a week in a relaxed but progression-focussed environment and we’ve got plenty of social members too. Outside of raid times, you’ll find us pushing mythic+ dungeons, doing transmog runs, going for glory in 3v3 arenas and helping each other out with quests.

Our raid times are:
Wednesdays 19.30 - 22.00
Sundays 19.30 - 22.00
All times are server time so check your time zone!

We’re not an elitist group and we don’t have a fixed raid composition - all are welcome so long as you’re keen, willing to commit and improve, and aren’t put off by a wipe or two. While high item levels and every Curve possible are great to have, we’re open to new raiders and are more interested in enthusiasm and commitment.

If you’re interested in joining Out of Mana, either as a raider or a social member, please take a look at our rules and FAQs then fill in an application.

All classes and specs are welcome, but we are currently particularly looking for ranged and melee dps. Tank and healer off-specs are also desirable.
