Rules

  • No swearing
  • No hate comments
  • Don't beg or insult others
  • Don't fight or spam in chat
  • Don't ask for boosts

Guidelines

Guild Chat

  • Say "Hi" and "Bye" when logging
  • Announce when going AFK
  • No shouting (excessive use of caps lock)
  • No spamming for help; if someone can help they will
  • Guild chat is not trade chat. WTS [Item] is banned
  • Offering rare items to guild members instead of selling is encouraged
  • Please reply to questions; "no thanks" is a lot nicer than feeling ignored

Activities

  • Try to participate in guild activities
  • You can create events on the calendar so long as it is not at guild raid time
  • Before queuing for anything it's nice to announce in guild chat to see if anyone wants to join

Conflicts

  • Attempt to resolve conflict amicably in private chat with the other party before escalating to an officer
  • Don't bring conflict into guild chat

Social

  • Call players by their name not their class
  • No elitist comments but helpful, constructive comments are encouraged

Discord

  • Use the same nickname as you're known in the guild
  • Use the raid night channel to let us know if you're going to be late if you can't access the in game calendar
  • We require push to talk for most of our voice channels
