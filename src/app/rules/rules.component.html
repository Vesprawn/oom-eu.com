Rules

  • Don't be a dick.
  • Swearing is strongly discouraged.
  • No hate comments/jokes of any kind.
  • Don't beg or insult other players, in or out of Out of Mana.
  • Don't fight or "Anal-Spam" in trade chat.

Guidlines

General
  • Please inform an officer if you're going to be away for longer than a month, to avoid being kicked.
  • Asking for boosts is discouraged.
Guild Chat
  • Say "Hi" and "Bye" when logging.
  • Announce when going AFK.
  • No shouting (exsessive use of caps lock).
  • No spamming for help, if someone can help they will.
  • Guild chat is not trade chat. WTS [Item] is banned.
  • Please reply to questions, "no thanks" is a lot nicer than feeling ignored.
Activities
  • Try to participate in guild activities.
  • Before queuing for anything it's nice to announce in guild chat if anyone wants to join.
Raiding
  • Pugging current raid content is frowned upon. Please ask an officer first.
Conflicts
  • Attempt to resolve conflict amicably in private chat with the other party before escalating to an officer.
  • Don't bring conflict into guild chat.
Social
  • Call players by their name not their class.
  • No elitist comments, constructive comments are encouraged.
